The Hidden Lifes of Trees



About the book
Dive into the enchanting, interconnected world of trees as forester Peter Wohlleben uncovers their secrets.
Using compelling storytelling and scientific insights, Wohlleben reveals how trees communicate through underground networks, support their neighbors, and even exhibit social behaviors.
This book inspires readers to view forests not just as collections of trees but as vibrant, thriving communities.
A fascinating read for nature lovers and environmental enthusiasts alike.


Braiding Sweetgrass



About the book
Combining Indigenous wisdom with scientific knowledge, Robin Wall Kimmerer invites readers into a deep exploration of our relationship with nature.
This thought-provoking and lyrical book uses stories about plants, animals, and ecosystems to highlight lessons about reciprocity, gratitude, and respect for the Earth.
Kimmerer’s dual perspective as a botanist and an Indigenous person creates a unique and profoundly moving narrative about sustainability and living harmoniously with nature.
