A Brief History of Time by Stephen Hawking



About the book
Stephen Hawking takes readers on an extraordinary journey through the cosmos in this groundbreaking exploration of time, space, and the universe.
Simplifying complex concepts of black holes, the Big Bang, and quantum mechanics, Hawking makes the wonders of the universe accessible to all.
Packed with insight and profound questions, this classic work bridges the gap between the scientific and the philosophical.
A must-read for anyone curious about the mysteries of our existence.


The Selfish Gene by Richard Dawkins



About the book
Richard Dawkins redefines the way we view evolution and natural selection in this revolutionary book.
Focusing on the gene as the central unit of selection, Dawkins explains how selfish genes drive survival and reproduction.
With clarity and wit, he provides fresh insights into human behavior, altruism, and evolution itself.
A landmark text that continues to shape how we understand life on Earth.
